W42ST is a magazine, a Facebook page, Twitter and Instagram feeds and more besides – holding all these elements together are some people. Our social media is updated constantly and is commented by the residents of Hell’s Kitchen whose lives we are on a mission to improve… through information, entertainment, through words and pictures.
